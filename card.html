Rabbit

Rabbits are small mammals with whiskers and distinctive long ears. Its tail is fluffy, short and round and it is called a bob. Rabbit is one of the most popular pets, after dog and cat.

  • Scientific Namw: Rakan
  • Length: 20cm
  • Lifespan: 2 year
  • Habitat: saudi arabia

Rabbits have a near 360-degree vision and can even see behind them. They have just one blind spot right in front of their nose.Their eyes are made for safety, too, since each eye can rotate 360 degrees. This allows them to look behind them without turning their heads
